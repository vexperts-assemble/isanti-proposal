Executive Summary

Background

VEX is excited to present two tailored solutions for redesigning the Isanti Glass website, each harnessing cutting-edge technology to showcase your premium glass products and streamline client interactions. Our proposals blend innovative design, artificial intelligence, and seamless integrations to position Isanti Glass as a leader in the industry, delivering value to both your team and your customers.

Implementation Options

Basic Implementation

R150,000 CAPEX

This cost-effective option lays a solid foundation for your digital evolution. Powered by Strapi's flexible content management system (with MySQL) and Caisera.ai's Standard tier for minimal yet impactful chatbot scenarios—such as answering FAQs and engaging new leads—this solution offers a responsive website with simplified AR/3D product previews (via Three.js) and a secure client portal (using Firebase Authentication). It's an ideal starting point for a lean, efficient online presence that meets essential needs while keeping future scalability in sight.

View Pricing Details

Full Implementation

R899,000 CAPEX

For those ready to fully embrace the future, this comprehensive package delivers a premium digital experience. Leveraging Strapi's robust CMS capabilities and Caisera.ai's Professional or Premium tier—supporting advanced scenarios like AI-driven sales cycles and detailed customer support—this option includes a visually stunning website with real-time AR/3D previews (powered by Unity) and an enhanced client portal (featuring Firebase Authentication with 2FA and a custom UI). It's a bold investment in innovation, designed to captivate clients, boost conversions, and solidify Isanti Glass's market dominance.
